HackCville's Photography Program

12 weeks: Febuary 6 - April 27

Learn digital photography & editing.

Start

Perfect for anyone interested in storytelling
through photography.

LEARN MORE→

Program Details

1. Learn photography using Nikon and Canon DSLRs.

2. Do projects in portraiture, landscape, journalistic photography.

3. Complete three short-term and one semester-long project to be published on personal photography portfolio website. See syllabus here.

4. Meets twice a week:

- Mondays 6-7:30 pm
- Wednesdays 2-3:30 pm

5. There is a $40 materials fee due at the beginning of the program. Financial assistance offered. Read materials fee for more info.
